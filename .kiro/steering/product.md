# The Military Taxi Company Website

A professional, modern website for a military-owned taxi company operating in the UK. The site emphasizes military precision combined with professional service, targeting customers who need reliable transportation services including airport transfers, city tours, and corporate travel.

## Key Brand Elements
- **Theme**: Military precision meets professional service
- **Colors**: Yellow and black professional taxi branding
- **Target Market**: UK-wide customers needing reliable taxi services
- **Service Areas**: Airports, seaports, tours, long distance, business travel, military bases
- **USP**: 24/7 availability, licensed & insured, 5-star rated service

## Core Features
- Online booking system with modal interface
- Responsive design for all devices
- Contact integration (phone/email)
- Service showcase with pricing
- Customer testimonials
- Professional military-focused branding
